About me
I am currently a junior at Lowell High School in San Francisco interested in the numerous fields within computer science and the applications of computer systems to real-world problems and innovations.
 
Because of my interest in these fields, I started numerous clubs and organizations in order to not only help further pursue these interests but also spread them to my peers and other students. Through this, I am exposed to many different types of people and it's always wonderful and fun being able to spread my love for science and technology to other people. My peers would describe me as proactive, driven, and resourceful who ensures a high level of quality and integrity in everything that I do. I always enjoy learning new things, generating new ideas and solutions, and solving problems.
